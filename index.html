The JTAV Experience

Joie’s philosophy is that no matter your age or gender, clean skin is the foundation of beautiful skin. That’s why all services begin with an extraction. While it may be time-consuming, an extraction is a best practice that makes everything else that comes after more effective. With a service menu that offers everything from Pore Cleansing to Ultherapy and more, Joie wants her clientele to experience lasting results that go further than what you see in the mirror.

Living in the Big City can be a taxing experience — on face, body, and mind. JTAV offers New Yorkers advanced aesthetic skin and body treatments, while at the same time, a respite from the world they give so much of themselves to. To this end, JTAV offers a luxurious environment, complete with advanced modalities, heated beds and luxe linens, so men and women are nourished not only on the outside, but on the inside. This way, their true beauty can shine brightly from within for all the world to see.
